Four branches. One tree.

Every calisthenics move you'll ever chase falls into one of these four skill branches.

💪

Push

Push-ups to planche. Build pressing strength through dips, handstands and straight-arm work.

  • Push-ups
  • Dips
  • Handstand
  • Planche
🧗

Pull

Rows to muscle-ups. Develop back and grip strength from your first pull-up to a front lever.

  • Rows
  • Pull-ups
  • Front Lever
  • Muscle-up
🦵

Legs

Squats to pistols. Build unilateral leg strength, mobility and explosive power from the ground up.

  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Pistol Squat
  • Shrimp Squat
🔥

Core

Plank to dragon flag. Train the isometric core control that holds every other skill together.

  • Plank
  • Hollow Body
  • L-Sit
  • Dragon Flag
